# ProdLytics Global Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** April 2, 2026  
**Canonical Public URL:** `https://prodlytics.vercel.app/privacy-policy`

---

## 1. Introduction & Scope
This Privacy Policy Outlines how **ProdLytics** ("we," "our," or "the Service") collects, processes, and protects your information across the ProdLytics Chrome extension and the related web application. We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of transparency regarding your data and productivity insights.

---

## 2. Information We Collect
To provide accurate productivity analytics, the Service processes the following data types:

### 2.1 Extension-Specific Data
- **Domain Activity:** We monitor active browser tab hostnames and page titles to categorize your time (e.g., distinguishing productive vs. distracting sites).
- **Usage Metrics:** Duration of active sessions, scroll frequency, and intermittent engagement signals used to determine active vs. idle states.
- **Technical Identifiers:** Extension version, browser type, and operating system for compatibility and bug reporting.

### 2.2 Account & Authentication Data
- **Identity Information:** If you register via Google Auth, we store your email address and profile name to sync data across devices.
- **Session Tokens:** Locally stored cryptographic tokens used to authenticate your extension with your private dashboard.

---

## 3. Purpose of Processing
Your data is processed strictly for the following functional purposes:
- **Productivity Analytics:** Generating personalized heatmaps and trend reports.
- **AI Insights:** Analyzing habit patterns to suggest optimal focus times.
- **Service Functionality:** Enforcing custom Focus Mode site blocks and delivering goal notifications.

**Note:** We do **NOT** sell your browsing history to third-party data brokers or use your activity for targeted advertising.

---

## 4. Data Storage & Retention
- **Local Cache:** High-frequency tracking data is stored in your `chrome.storage.local` area and is cleared upon extension uninstallation.
- **Cloud Storage:** Synchronized data is stored on secure cloud servers. We retain summarized data as long as your account remains active.

---

## 5. Security Measures
We implement industry-standard security measures, including **TLS/SSL encryption** for data in transit and **at-rest encryption** for cloud databases. Access to user data is strictly logged and restricted to essential service maintenance.

---

## 6. Third-Party Services
ProdLytics utilizes trusted infrastructure providers (e.g., Vercel, MongoDB) and authentication services (e.g., Google OAuth). These providers are restricted from using your data for any unauthorized purposes.

---

## 7. Global User Rights (GDPR/CCPA)
Regardless of your location, we aim to provide global standards of data autonomy:
- **Right of Access:** View all tracked activity directly via the ProdLytics dashboard.
- **Right to Erasure:** Request full account and tracking data deletion via the contact information below.
- **Portability:** Request an export of your productivity history in a machine-readable format.

---

## 8. Children's Privacy
ProdLytics is not directed at individuals under the age of 13. If we discover accidental collection, we will take immediate steps to delete the data.
