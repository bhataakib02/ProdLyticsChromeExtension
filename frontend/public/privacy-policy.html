ProdLytics Global Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy Outlines how ProdLytics ("we," "our," or "the Service") collects, processes, and protects your information across the ProdLytics Chrome extension and the related web application. We are committed to transparency regarding your data and productivity insights.

1. Information We Collect

To provide accurate productivity analytics, the Service processes the following data types:

  • Domain Activity: We monitor active browser tab hostnames and page titles to categorize your time (e.g., distinguishing productive vs. distracting sites).
  • Usage Metrics: Duration of active sessions, scroll frequency, and intermittent engagement signals used to determine active vs. idle states.
  • Identity Information: If you register via Google Auth, we store your email address and profile name to sync data across devices.
  • Technical Identifiers: Extension version, browser type, and encrypted session tokens used for authentication.

2. Purpose of Processing

Your data is processed strictly for the following functional purposes:

  • Generating personalized productivity heatmaps and trend reports for your dashboard.
  • AI-driven habit analysis to suggest optimal focus times.
  • Enforcing custom Focus Mode site blocks and delivering goal notifications.

Note: We do NOT sell your browsing history to third-party data brokers or use your activity for targeted advertising.

3. Data Storage & Retention

  • High-frequency tracking data is stored in your chrome.storage.local area and is cleared upon uninstallation.
  • Synchronized data is stored on secure cloud servers. We retain summarized data as long as your account remains active.

4. Security Protocols

We implement industry-standard security measures, including TLS/SSL encryption for data in transit and at-rest encryption for cloud databases. Access to user data is strictly logged and limited to essential maintenance.

5. Third-Party Integration

We utilize trusted infrastructure providers (e.g., Vercel, MongoDB) and authentication services (e.g., Google OAuth). These providers are contractually restricted from using your data for any unauthorized purposes.

6. User Rights & Data Autonomy

Regardless of your jurisdiction (including GDPR/CCPA contexts), you have the right to:

  • Access: View all tracked activity directly via the ProdLytics dashboard.
  • Erasure: Request full account and tracking data deletion via the contact information below.
  • Portability: Request a export of your productivity history in a machine-readable format.

7. Children's Privacy

ProdLytics is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. If we discover accidental data collection from a child, we will take immediate steps to delete that information.
