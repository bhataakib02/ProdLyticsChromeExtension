ProdLytics Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

This policy describes how the ProdLytics Chrome extension and related ProdLytics web application (“Services”) handle information. By using the Services, you agree to this policy.

1. Who we are

ProdLytics is a productivity tool that helps you understand how you spend time in the browser and manage focus goals. The extension works together with the ProdLytics dashboard website.

2. What we collect

2.1 Browsing activity (extension)

When you use the extension, it may process:

  • Hostname (and related URL context) of the sites you visit while the extension is active
  • Time spent on those sites (aggregated into sessions)
  • Page title (where available), used to help categorize sites
  • Light engagement signals (e.g. scroll and click counts) for insights—not for advertising

We do not sell your browsing history. We do not use it to show third-party ads inside the extension.

2.2 Account and authentication

  • You may sign in (e.g. with Google) or receive an anonymous session tied to your browser.
  • The extension may store a session token locally to sync with your account on the ProdLytics backend.

2.3 Optional notifications

The extension may show Chrome notifications (e.g. focus reminders or goal completion). You can limit these in Chrome’s extension settings.

3. Where data is stored

  • Synced data is stored on servers operated for ProdLytics (your deployed backend / database).
  • Some data stays locally in Chrome storage (cached goals, blocklist, session token).

The exact region depends on your cloud provider; operators may disclose it via NEXT_PUBLIC_DATA_REGION on the dashboard deployment.

4. Who can see your data

  • You see your data in the dashboard.
  • Operators may access data only as needed to run, secure, and improve the service.
  • We do not intentionally share browsing history with unrelated third parties for their marketing.

If you use Google Sign-In, Google’s privacy policy applies to that flow.

5. How we use data

  • Time and focus statistics in the dashboard
  • Goals, blocklists / focus mode, and insights you configure
  • Sync between extension and web app for the same account

6. Security

We use HTTPS and authentication tokens for API access. No method is 100% secure; protect your device and accounts.

7. Your choices

  • Uninstall the extension at any time.
  • Clear extension data in Chrome.
  • Disconnect from the dashboard; contact us below for data requests where applicable.
